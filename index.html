Frequently asked questions

How do I download a document?

Select the Download button on any document card. The PDF will download or open depending on your browser settings.

How do I know if a policy is current?

Each document card includes an updated date. Staff should replace old PDFs whenever a policy is reviewed.

Can this portal be linked from an LMS?

Yes. Copy the GitHub Pages URL for this website and add it as a link inside Moodle, Canvas, Blackboard or another LMS.
