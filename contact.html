Frequently Asked Questions

Who should I contact about a missing document?

Contact Student Support and include the document name, course name and LMS page where the link appears.

Can students access this portal on mobile?

Yes. The portal is fully responsive and works on mobile, tablet and desktop devices.

Does this website store student information?

No. This is a static website and does not use a database. The support form opens an email request only.
